Tournament Format:

The tournament is conducted in two stages:

qualification



The qualifying phase will last for a maximum of one month from the start of the tournament.
If 75% of the participants have played their third games, then the countdown for the last four days until the end of the qualification begins (they must be completed no later than the date specified above). Therefore, it is not allowed to schedule additional games on days after the announced date. Games that have started or continued on the last allowable day of qualifying games will be played without any additional saves.


play-off



Players who ranked from 1st to 21st in the qualification stage and have at least one full stream are eligible to participate in the playoffs.
In all playoff matches, the slots are distributed according to the qualification results. (LOOK ABOVE) The nations are determined in the same way as in the qualifying stage. It is not allowed to change the set of nations. Exactly one week is allocated for each round of the playoffs, and each player must check in to the corresponding event within two days to avoid being replaced.
