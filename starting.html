Procedure for finding games

Time for games

By default, game time is considered to be from 7:00 pm to 2:00 am. Of course, this does not mean that you are required to play only from seven in the evening until two in the morning, but if you cannot play during this period (or most of it), it is better not to participate in the tournament.
Players are free to agree on any convenient time for them..
All specified times are considered according to Athens time UTC +3 , unless otherwise agreed.
If the game lasts more than 6 hours from the start, players can request a save and continuation of the game (if not otherwise specified in the event).

Creating and conducting your own event in the qualification round

You create an event (Thread) in discord game section with the name "TournamentGame - YourNicknameInSteam". From this moment, you become the organizer of this game, and you have corresponding obligations, the successful fulfillment of which will earn you a bonus point. In the event, you set the start time of the game and indicate when the save will be made (if planned)

Keep track of who is posting in your event.

For the first five people who post, you create a game. That is, you do not choose those whom you like more, but you must play with the first five who posted. Two exceptions when choosing players among those who posted:

You must ensure that the conditions for playing with repeat players are met for all participants. In case of a violation, priority is given to those who posted earlier.

Once you have selected five opponents, choose a free room on our Discord server, invite all participants there, and discuss all the details more specifically there..

Organize the process of choosing nations (see below).

Who will physically host the game is proposed to be decided by the players themselves, based on the internet and computer capabilities of each of them. By default, this is the event organizer.

Joining an existing event in the qualifying round

Find the tournament event titled "TournamentGame-OrganizerName". In games Tournament section of Discord

Read the conditions written by the event organizer.

Check if any of the players you have played with in past tournament games have already signed up. If so, determine if the rules allow you to play with repeat players.

If you agree to the conditions, mark yourself as participating in the event (usually done with a "+"). This indicates that you are willing to participate under the proposed conditions.

Add the event organizer as a friend and wait for them to invite you to the Discord room. In the Discord room, discuss all the details, write down bans for nations (if applicable), and more.

When saving the game, do not wait for the host to invite you to play. Show initiative and remind the host about the game.

You are allowed to mark yourself as participating in no more than two events simultaneously. . However, if you decide to participate in two events, you must notify each event that you have signed up for the other. It is assumed that you will play in the event that gathers sooner.

Having more than two unfinished games is prohibited

Note: having a third unfinished game is considered problematic because games are expected to be completed within four days by default..

Conducting Playoff Matches

In playoff matches, unlike in the qualifying round, the participants and their slots are known in advance. The player in the first slot is responsible for the event organization.

Nations Draft

Nations are determined only by the method described below. Each participant bans 2 nations.

Each player in the game is randomly assigned 3 nations.

To determine nations, you need to enter the command "!b civ1 civ2" in the "tournament draft" channel on our Discord server, list the nations for the ban and the Discord usernames of the players participating in the game. For example: "@player1 !b Arabia Babylon @player2 !b Korea Poland @player3 !b Zulu Aztec, etc..."

It is forbidden to change the set of nations that have been assigned

You are not allowed to play the same nation again. The event organizer must ensure compliance with this rule. If a player receives all the nations they have already played, the draw is repeated (the nation bans remain the same).

Restarts and canceling a game due to technical issues

Canceling a game due to technical issues is only possible before turn 100. Indicators of technical problems include constant desyncs and/or crashes. Before canceling the game, the players must try loading the save, changing the host, or finding a replacement for the problematic player. The final decision is made after the event organizer contacts one of the tournament organizers. In case of game cancellation. However, the message about the start of the game and technical issues from the event organizer should still be in the tournament thread.

Rules of voice communication in Discord

You are required to behave respectfully towards all participants in the game. Insulting players and personal attacks are strictly prohibited.

You are required to LISTEN to all participants in the game. Muting others is not allowed.

You are responsible for everything that is said under your name on the stream.

The use of profanity and vulgar language during communication is prohibited. If you swear in a fit of emotion, it's understandable, but constantly using profanity in every sentence is not allowed. Remember that tournament games are content for the group and should attract new players.

It is prohibited for other players to be in the communication room during the game, except for the participants. An exception is when you are doing a review of the game. In this case, you must remain silent in the room, and it is strictly forbidden to ask participants questions, engage in conversation, and similar activities. Also, persons who are reviewing the game are not allowed to write to other players even in the general chat! (since you know more about the game than the player). It is also prohibited to encourage other viewers to write your questions/ideas in the general chat.

IMPORTANT! During the game, you are strictly prohibited from entering the "Analytics Studio" room. If, for example, you want to join the conversation or call one of the judges to resolve a disputed situation, you should write a personal message to the judges.

If there are claims from at least one player, it is prohibited to have a conversation on topics unrelated to the game (= no "spam"). The following ideas are prohibited in diplomacy: war until the end of the game (="suiciding"). Of course, you should distinguish the situation when players simply do not reconcile until the end of the game. playing exclusively against one particular player, for example, "I won't let you win". Do not confuse with the situation when the point was that "I won't let you win just like that".

Post-match voting. Players are not allowed to mention or include it in any diplomatic matters during the game. Speeches like "let's make a deal, you vote for me and I'll vote for you higher in the voting if you don't win" are strictly prohibited.

Inviting to be conquered. Except for intentional provocation, phrases like "go ahead, conquer me, I don't care anymore, I'll go to sleep" or "I won't resist" or "I'll delete my units" are strictly prohibited to say and even more so to carry out.

Conceding the game:
You can propose a distribution of places (specifically a distribution, not just saying who won), which ends the game if it is unanimously accepted. So, you can say "let's vote for Inca to win, second place for me, third for China, fourth for Assyria, if you agree, put a plus sign in the chat, if you disagree, put a minus sign" (someone may propose a different option and players also vote), but you cannot say "let's recognize Inca's victory and end the game". During the voting for a distribution of places, it is not allowed to influence the voters (for example, you cannot say "otherwise, I'll come after you") - ideally, it should take place in silence. Of course, players can propose their own options for distribution.

Any condemnation, criticism, or other negative remarks towards a player who does not agree with the distribution (even if they are the only one) are prohibited.

Talking about which player is winning, going towards victory, or leaving the game is, of course, allowed.

When is it okay to leave a game?

A player has the right to leave the game in the following situations:
the game has not entered its final stage >info era: the player can leave after losing (or possibly recapturing) their capital within 10 turns. If the game has entered its final stage(info era) , in addition to losing their capital, the player also needs the consent of all other players to leave the game. If the player has not lost their capital, but is in last place in all key indicators with a significant lag, they can initiate a vote by writing "irr" in the game's general chat. The vote is only valid during this turn. To leave, they need the agreement of absolutely all other players
(who vote in the game's general chat with "+" or "-"). This option can be used before the game enters its final stage, but no more than once every 20 turns.

How and when can a game be ended?

Let's call the turn in which one of the players entered the Information Era the turn from which the game entered the final stage. All players who were in the game at the time of the start of this turn are considered participants in the final stage.

If a player left the game or was eliminated before the final stage, they will take the lowest place among the remaining players.

All the players who have reached the final stage of the game (regardless of further developments in the game) participate in the vote to determine the final rankings.

If the game ended with one of the players winning before the final stage, then all players who were in the lobby at the time of the end of the game participate in the vote..

You are required to send me (aggressor) your final rankings within 24 hours after the end of the game. In case a player suddenly disappears or is disqualified, one of the members of the judging committee will vote instead of him/her.

For each player, the average arithmetic mean will be calculated among all received scores, excluding one minimum and one maximum.

You have the right to end the game after 100 turns, but before entering the final stage, if all players agree on the distribution of places. Then each player receives points without additional calculations according to their place. If the players do not come to an agreement on the distribution of places, the game continues until one of the players wins.

After the game enters the final stage and all players are still in the game, you can end the game by unanimous agreement on the distribution of places, or declare a winner and the remaining places will be distributed by voting.

If a game has entered its final stage and at least one player has been eliminated since the start of the final stage, the remaining players can unanimously agree to recognize one player as the winner, and the other places will be distributed by a vote.

If there are only two players left in the game, one of them can always surrender. If this happens in the final stage of the game, the surrendering player participates in the vote. If it happens before the final stage, the surrendering player takes the second place.
