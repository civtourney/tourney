Registration Process

General Information

The tournament starts after X people register (but not earlier than X Date). If there are less than X participants by X Date, the tournament will be canceled.

Participation Criteria

You must have an open profile on steam (available for viewing by everyone, not just friends).All game DlC are required. During the tournament, you cannot change your nickname in Steam, Twitch, and Discord.. When registering for the tournament, the organizer may appoint a Discord chat, after which they have the right to refuse participation without explanation. During this conversation, the player's adequacy, knowledge of the game, and ability to communicate by voice are checked.

What you need to participate

You need to add the tournament organizer, aggressor , as a friend to send your votes with place distribution in the match. Also, to promptly report any necessary delays or problems in the party. IMPORTANT! To register for the tournament, you must write in the Registration section of the Discord group. Wait for approval from the tournament organizers or a message with a list of what needs to be corrected. After receiving approval, your nickname will be added to the list of tournament participants.
